Who We Are

Gaur Legal Service is composed of a team of highly skilled and experienced lawyers dedicated to protecting our clients' rights. Our lawyers are experts in diverse areas of law, and our firm handles various types of legal cases, ranging from civil disputes to corporate law, human rights, criminal defense, family matters, and constitutional issues. We are committed to providing reliable legal advice with integrity, transparency, and compassion.

Our Legal Services

We offer a wide range of legal services across multiple areas of practice. Our legal experts specialize in:

  • Litigation Services: Representing clients in civil and criminal courts, including case preparation, filing of petitions, and court representation.
  • Corporate Law: Advising businesses on compliance, mergers, acquisitions, and drafting contracts and agreements to safeguard corporate interests.
  • Family Law: Handling divorce, child custody, property disputes, and other family-related legal matters.
  • Human Rights Law: Defending the fundamental rights of individuals against violations, ensuring justice for marginalized and vulnerable communities.
  • Real Estate Law: Providing legal assistance on property transactions, disputes, and regulatory compliance regarding real estate development and sales.
  • Constitutional Law: Advising clients on constitutional matters, including fundamental rights, public interest litigation (PIL), and judicial review.
  • Criminal Defense: Representing individuals accused of criminal offenses, providing a robust defense to ensure fair trials and just outcomes.
  • Corporate Compliance: Helping businesses stay compliant with local, national, and international laws, preventing legal issues before they arise.
